Overview

The Department of Commerce at the University of the Punjab Gujranwala Campus aims to equip students with a solid foundation in commerce and business principles.

Program Aims

Our mission is to foster critical thinking and analytical skills essential for success in the dynamic world of commerce.

Curriculum

The Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) program focuses on key areas such as accounting, finance, marketing, and management.

Extracurricular Activities

The department is committed to enhancing the learning experience through various initiatives, including workshops, seminars, and guest lectures by industry professionals.

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the program are well-prepared for diverse career opportunities in both the private and public sectors, contributing to the economic growth of the nation.
